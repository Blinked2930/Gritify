# The Daily Vault: 225 Questions (Sample Structure)

*The engine selects 3 questions per day: 1 Physical, 1 Mind, 1 Albania/Goofy.*

### Category: Physical (The Grind)
1. If your sweat today was a perfume, what would it be called?
2. Which muscle is currently yelling the loudest at you?
3. What's the "Main Character" song of your outdoor workout today?

### Category: Mind (The 10 Pages)
4. If you had to explain today’s reading to a golden retriever, what would you say?
5. What’s one sentence you read today that changed your brain chemistry?
6. Is your brain currently a 'Zettelkasten' or a 'junk drawer'?

### Category: Albania & Goofy (The Connection)
7. What did the host family serve that Gael definitely wouldn't have eaten?
8. If you could trade your gallon of water for one local snack right now, what is it?
9. Rate today's "Vibe" from 1 (Cold Shower) to 10 (Fresh Byrek).
